Services

Kitty C4 offers a wide variety of services! In every category, there are three levels of quality (all amazing, of course), which you can use to customize your own price and type of service!

Grooming:

  • Silver: we pet the cat
  • Gold: we brush the cat
  • Platinum: we bathe the cat in a luxury essential oil bubble bath

Entertaining:

  • Silver: we have a staring contest with the cat
  • Gold: we throw a toy for the cat
  • Platinum: we help the cat hunt local mice in the neighborhood

Training

  • Silver: we potty train the cat
  • Gold: we train the cat to ring a bell when it wants food
  • Platinum: we teach the cat how to operate a can opener

Fitness

  • Silver: we give the cat a pep talk
  • Gold: we take the cat for a walk
  • Platinum: we send the cat through a Ninja Warrior agility course
